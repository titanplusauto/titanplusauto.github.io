1. What to do in case your servers/databases are hacked?

We encrypt and store our clientsâ€™ seller authorization tokens in the database. If the database was hacked, we will notify our clients and request them to revoke our permissions to access their customer data. After the incident is investigated and closed, we will try to negotiate with Amazon to get a new pair of access key and secret key.

2. What to do in case an unauthorized access to customer data is detected?

Customer data are not stored or displayed anywhere in database or application.

3. Who to contact in case of an incident and what steps to follow?

In case of an incident, all computers will be taken offline and isolated. After the breach is contained, we will assess the damage and notify Amazon with 24 hours. Based on the type of data compromised, we may notify our clients what happened and steps they can do for protection from any further damage. Next, we will investigate what went wrong and how to prevent it from happening again. After new measures are implemented, we will resume operation.

4. What to do in case your servers leaked Amazon Information?

We only have a database server and accessible only from our internal network. If this server is compromised, we will follow the steps listed in the answer for question 1.

5. How to reach out to Amazon to inform them of the incident?

We will notify Amazon at security@amazon.com
